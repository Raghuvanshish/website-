Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

Data Science and Artificial Intelligence were established in the year 2021 in Himachal Pradesh University (HPU). It provides an opportunity for all university students to develop AI skills and learn through computer aided learning process. So it is a major catalyst to bridge the digital divide amongst students of various socioeconomic and other geographical barriers.
Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence in the Himachal Pradesh University was established during the session 2020-21. The laboratory is equipped with twenty-five computers with computer tables and chairs. The lab has a LAN connection and all computers are connected through proper networking. Also University campus provided the free Wifi to all the students. It also has two all-in-one printers. The lab also has a mounted LCD projector along with a white screen.
It is used by Department faculty for planning and other works related to university theory and practical examination. It is also used for work related to online admissions and scholarship.
