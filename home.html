ABOUT DEPARTMENT OF DATA SCIENCE AND ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

The Department of Data Science & Artificial Intelligence at Himachal Pradesh University in Shimla is a dynamic and innovative hub of learning in the ever-evolving fields of data science and artificial intelligence. With a commitment to excellence, our department has established itself as a leading center of academic and technological advancement. Our department is dedicated to empowering students with a deep understanding of data science and artificial intelligence.
